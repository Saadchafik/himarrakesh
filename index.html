🐪 Merzouga · 3 Days / 2 Nights

Merzouga Desert — Luxury Camp & Quad

Highlights:
  • Cross the High Atlas Mountains and Tizi n'Tichka Pass
  • Visit Ait Ben Haddou Kasbah (UNESCO site)
  • Explore Todra Gorges and Dades Valley
  • Overnight stay in luxury desert camp in Merzouga
  • Quad bike experience in the Sahara Desert
  • Camel ride or 4x4 transfer in Erg Chebbi dunes
  • Sunset and sunrise in Merzouga Desert
  • Traditional dinner and Berber music
Day 1: Marrakech – Atlas – Ait Ben Haddou – Dades

Early pickup, cross High Atlas Mountains via Tizi n'Tichka Pass. Visit UNESCO Ait Ben Haddou Kasbah. Continue through Ouarzazate and Valley of Roses to Dades Valley. Dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Dades – Todra – Merzouga – Luxury Camp

Visit Todra Gorges. Head to Merzouga and enter Erg Chebbi dunes by camel or 4x4. Quad biking adventure in Sahara. Traditional dinner, Berber music, stargazing.

Day 3: Merzouga – Draa Valley – Marrakech

Sunrise over dunes. Return through scenic Draa Valley and Atlas Mountains. Arrive Marrakech evening.

€110 1 200 DH
